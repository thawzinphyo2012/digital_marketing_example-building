Answers

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about timelines, deliverables, and how we run Facebook and TikTok work with you.

How long does a Facebook or TikTok campaign take to launch? +

After assets and access are ready, most monthly content plans go live within 1–2 weeks. Video production batches depend on scripts and shoot days — we agree milestones in the kickoff call.

Can you adapt existing brand guidelines? +

Yes. We can extend established grids, color, and type systems while keeping production partners aligned on specs and exports.

Do you work with clients outside Myanmar? +

Yes. We run remote-first collaboration — shared calendars, cloud folders, and structured async updates so campaigns stay on schedule.

Do you collaborate with developers or printers? +

Often. We supply annotated files, asset naming, and export presets so implementation or print production stays smooth.

What files are included at handoff? +

Organized Figma libraries, PDF brand guidelines, print-ready exports, and motion references when applicable.

What do you need from me to get started? +

A short brief, links to references you like, audience notes, and any must-keep constraints (logos, legal, accessibility).

How do revisions and rounds of feedback work? +

Each phase includes agreed revision rounds (typically two per milestone). Extra rounds can be scoped as a small add-on so timelines stay predictable.

How do payments and deposits work? +

A deposit secures the calendar; remaining invoices are tied to agreed milestones. Terms are confirmed in writing before work begins.

What is your pricing structure? +

Projects are usually fixed-fee by phase with a clear scope document. For exploratory work, a discovery sprint can be quoted separately.

What if the project scope changes mid-way? +

We pause, document the new scope, and agree on timeline and fee adjustments before continuing — no surprise scope creep.
